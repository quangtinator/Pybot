# Travel Bucket List & Restaurant Guide

## Paris

- **Must Eat:**
  - Boulangerie Utopie (Croissants)
  - Le Relais de l'Entrecôte (Steak Frites)
  - Pierre Hermé (Macarons)
- **Must See:**
  - Sainte-Chapelle (Need to book tickets 2 weeks prior)
  - Musée d'Orsay
  - Palace of Versailles (Day trip - taking the RER C train)

## Tokyo

- **Must Eat:**
  - Ichiran Sushi (Shibuya)
  - Gyukatsu Motomura (Need to line up early!)
  - Any FamilyMart for Egg Sando
- **Must See:**
  - TeamLab Planets (Digital Art Museum)
  - Senso-ji Temple early morning
  - Ghibli Museum (Tickets sell out 1st of month prior)

## London

- **Must Eat:**
  - Dishoom (Indian food)
  - Borough Market (Assorted street food)
  - Fish & Chips at The Mayfair Chippy
- **Must See:**
  - British Museum
  - Sky Garden
  - West End Show (Phantom of the Opera or Wicked)

## Sydney

- **Must Eat:**
  - Quay Restaurant (Fine dining with Opera House view)
  - Sydney Fish Market (Fresh oysters)
  - Harry's Cafe de Wheels (Meat pies)
- **Must See:**
  - Sydney Opera House (Take the interior tour)
  - Bondi to Coogee Coastal Walk
  - Taronga Zoo

## Rome

- **Must Eat:**
  - Roscioli Salumeria con Cucina (Best Carbonara)
  - Giolitti (Cacio e Pepe)
  - Any authentic Gelateria near the Pantheon
- **Must See:**
  - Colosseum & Roman Forum (Underground tour)
  - Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel (Skip the line passes)
  - Trevi Fountain (Go early morning to avoid crowds)
